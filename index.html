carne de res guisada / beef stew

There really is nothing like a sweet, savory, and tender piece of meet that just bursts with flavor, and hits just right. This way of cooking meat has long been my favorite way to cook and season to get that perfect flavor and tenderness.

箝進ngredients:

  • 21 lbs cubed steak
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 green pepper sliced
  • 3-4 tbs sofrito
  • 1 1/2 tbs chicken bullion/ adobo
  • 1/2 tablespoon oregano
  • 1 tbs sazon
  • 1/2 tbs sugar
  • 1 tbs tomato paste
  • 1 lime
  • black pepper to taste/chilli oil
  • 1 tbs white vinigar

    • serve with dominican rice and beans

    pastelon / sweet plantain lasagnia

    I love lasagnia, but this is hands down the best way to make it. Instead of layering startchy thick pasta, Pastelon is layered with sweet mashed plantain, and topped with A LOT of cheese!! If you haven't tried it, this is your sign to do so! It's one of the simplest and easiest things to make, and it's a family favorite.

    箝進ngredients

  • 1lbs ground beef
  • 1 chopped red onion
  • 1 tsp of mashed garlic
  • 1/2 cubanelle pepper
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • oregano
  • thyme
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste

    • mash together:

  • 6 sweet plantain
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbs vegie oil
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 tbs whipping cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

    • assemble in oven dish and bake at 350 deg until cheese is melted
